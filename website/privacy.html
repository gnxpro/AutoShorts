Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GNX Production Studio ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our desktop software and services.

1. Information Collection & API Usage

GNX Production Studio integrates with third-party APIs including YouTube Data API, TikTok Content Posting API, and Meta Graph API to provide video automation and publishing features.

  • OAuth Data: We access your social media account information (username/channel name) and permissions to upload videos only after you grant explicit consent via the official OAuth login screen.
  • Content: The App processes video URLs and local media files provided by you to perform AI-driven tasks like cropping, subtitle generation, and hook creation.

2. Local Data Processing & Storage

Your privacy is our priority. Most processing occurs locally on your device.

  • Offline Tokens: All API Access Tokens and Refresh Tokens are stored locally on your personal computer (typically in the AppData folder). We do not store or transmit these tokens to our own servers.
  • No Password Storage: GNX Production Studio never asks for, collects, or stores your social media passwords.

3. Third-Party Services

When you use GNX to publish content, your data is subject to the privacy policies of the respective platforms:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Cloudinary Privacy Policy: cloudinary.com/privacy

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your local configuration files. You can revoke the App's access at any time through your Google, TikTok, or Meta account security settings.
