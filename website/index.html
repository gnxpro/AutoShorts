Privacy Policy

GNX Production Studio ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our YouTube integration features.

1. Data Collection: We use YouTube OAuth to allow you to upload videos to your own channel. We do not store your Google password. Your access tokens are stored locally on your computer and are never transmitted to our servers.

2. Usage: The application only accesses your YouTube account to: (a) Upload videos produced by you, and (b) Display your channel name in the dashboard for identification.

3. Data Protection: You can revoke access at any time by deleting the local 'token.json' file or via your Google Account security settings.
