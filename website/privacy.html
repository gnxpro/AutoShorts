Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

GNX Studio ("we," "us," or "our") is a desktop video automation application operated by PT GENERAL EXPLORER SMART SYSTEM. This Privacy Policy explains how GNX Studio accesses, processes, and stores user data when users connect supported third-party platforms such as YouTube, TikTok, and other social media services.

1. Local Data Processing (Crucial)

GNX Studio is a desktop application. Crucially, the vast majority of your data, including video content and generated API session tokens, are processed and stored locally on your own device. We do not automatically upload your video files or personal authentication tokens to our servers.

2. Information We Access and Collect via APIs

When you connect supported third-party platforms to GNX Studio, the application may request limited permissions required to provide login, account connection, and content publishing features.

  • YouTube: GNX Studio may request permissions such as channel access and upload-related scopes to enable YouTube publishing and channel management features.
  • TikTok: GNX Studio may request permissions needed for account authentication, basic account information access, and video upload features, depending on the capabilities enabled in the connected TikTok app.

3. Use of Information

We use the accessed information exclusively to enable the core functionalities of the App, which include automating video management and distribution.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

GNX Studio explicitly does not share, transfer, sell, or disclose your Google user data (including YouTube account information) to any third parties, advertising platforms, or data brokers. Your data is used exclusively within the application to perform your requested automated video publishing tasks.

5. Data Protection Mechanisms

We take the security of your sensitive information seriously. All sensitive data, including Google OAuth credentials and access tokens, are encrypted and stored locally on your personal device. GNX Studio does not transmit your authentication tokens to any external servers or remote databases.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

GNX Studio retains your Google user data locally on your device only for as long as you actively use the application. You maintain full control over your data. You can delete all Google user data from the application at any time by manually deleting the local tokens directory. Additionally, you can revoke the application's access entirely at any time via your Google Account Security settings page at myaccount.google.com/permissions .

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: gnx@gnxpro.my.id
